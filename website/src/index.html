Rives with Freedoom
PLAY AND SUBMIT LEADERBOARD

Rives is an onchain fantasy console powered by Cartesi Rollups. This version of Freedoom is an adaptation of the original DOOM game using FREEDOOM assets.

Instructions: You will only play a few levels. Every item, kill, secret or level increases your overall score, while every second decreases your score. What is the best score you can make? Controls: - LEFT/RIGHT/UP/DOWN - Move - CTRL/F/R2 - Shoot - SPACE/C/A3 - Use - SHIFT/Z/A1 - Run - ALT/A/L2 - Strafe - S/L1 - Prev weapon - D/R1 - Next weapon - TAB/START - Map
